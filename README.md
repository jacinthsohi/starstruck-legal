# Starstruck — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 15, 2026_

Starstruck ("we", "us") is a dining journal that lets you log Michelin-starred
restaurant visits and share them with friends. This policy explains what we
collect and why. Plain English, no surprises.

## What we collect

- **Account info.** When you sign in with Apple (or Google), we receive your
  email address and, on first sign-in, your name. We use this to create and
  secure your account.
- **Profile.** A **username** (your public handle), a display name, and an
  optional profile photo you choose to upload.
- **Your content.** The visits you log (restaurant, date, your rating and notes),
  photos you add to a visit, and the friends/companions you connect with or tag.
- **Social graph.** Your friend connections, dining-club memberships, and any
  users you block or report.
- **Notifications.** If you enable push notifications, a device token so we can
  send them.
- **Diagnostics.** Basic technical data needed to operate the app.

We do **not** track you across other apps or websites, and we do **not** sell
your data or use it for advertising.

## How we use it

Only to make the app work: to show your dining history and dashboard, to let you
find and connect with friends, to tag companions on visits, to share what you
choose with the people you choose, to send notifications you've enabled, and to
keep the community safe. That's it.

## Who can see your data — and your controls

- **You** can see all of your own data.
- **Your public profile card** — username, display name, and profile photo — can
  be seen by other users so they can find you and send a friend request. You
  control this with the **Discoverability** setting (findable by everyone, or not
  listed in search — invite-link only).
- **Friends** can see what you choose to share. In **Privacy & sharing** you can
  independently turn off your stats, badges, your visit list, and visit dates —
  each is enforced on our servers, not just hidden in the app.
- **Companions** on a visit can see that visit's photos and details.
- Your **notes and photos** are visible only to you and the confirmed companions
  on a visit — never to friends who weren't there.
- We use **Supabase** (our hosting provider) to store data securely. Access is
  protected by per-user database security rules.

## Safety & moderation

You can **block** any user (they can't find, add, or see you) and **report**
content or people. Reports go to our team, and we may remove content or suspend
accounts that break our Terms. See the [Terms of Service](terms-of-service.md).

## Sign in with Apple / Google

We use these only to authenticate you. If you choose to hide your email with
Apple's private relay, that's fully supported.

## Your choices and rights

- **Choose what you share** anytime in Account → Privacy & sharing (discoverability
  and per-category sharing).
- **Block or report** other users from their profile or a search result.
- **Edit or delete content** anytime in the app.
- **Delete your account** anytime from the Account screen ("Delete Account"). This
  permanently removes your account, visits, photos, connections, blocks, and the
  reports you filed.
- Questions or requests: **help@starstruck.club**.

## Optional features

- **Reservation import (opt-in).** You can connect Gmail to import restaurant
  reservations (e.g., Resy/Tock/OpenTable confirmations). This uses **read-only**
  access and only reads reservation-related messages. It's off unless you turn it
  on, and you can disconnect anytime.
- **Finding friends from contacts (future).** We may later let you find friends
  who are already on Starstruck. If we do, it will be opt-in and use privacy-
  preserving matching (we compare scrambled values, not your raw address book).
  We'll update this policy before launching it.

## Children

Starstruck is intended for users 17 and older and is not directed at children.

## Changes

We'll update this page and the "Last updated" date if this policy changes.
