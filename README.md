# Starstruck — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 24, 2026_

Starstruck ("we", "us") is a dining journal that lets you log Michelin-starred
restaurant visits and share them with friends. This policy explains what we
collect and why. Plain English, no surprises.

## What we collect

- **Account info.** When you sign in with Apple (or Google), we receive your
  email address and, on first sign-in, your name. We use this to create and
  secure your account.
- **Profile.** A display name and an optional profile photo you choose to upload.
- **Your content.** The visits you log (restaurant, date, your rating and notes),
  photos you add to a visit, and the friends/companions you connect with or tag.

We do **not** track you across other apps or websites, and we do **not** sell
your data or use it for advertising.

## How we use it

Only to make the app work: to show your dining history and dashboard, to let you
connect with friends, to tag companions on visits, and to share visit photos with
the people on that visit. That's it.

## Who can see your data

- **You** can see all of your own data.
- **Friends and companions** can see visits and photos you share with them (for
  example, a visit you tag them on). You control these connections.
- We use **Supabase** (our hosting provider) to store data securely. Access is
  protected by per-user database security rules.

## Sign in with Apple / Google

We use these only to authenticate you. If you choose to hide your email with
Apple's private relay, that's fully supported.

## Your choices and rights

- **Edit or delete content** anytime in the app.
- **Delete your account** anytime from the Account screen ("Delete Account"). This
  permanently removes your account, visits, photos, and connections.
- Questions or requests: **jacinthsohi@gmail.com**.

## Children

Starstruck is intended for users 17 and older and is not directed at children.

## Future features

A future version may offer to import restaurant reservations from your email
(e.g., Resy/Tock/OpenTable confirmations). This will be **opt-in**, will request
**read-only** email access, and will only read reservation-related messages. We'll
update this policy before launching it.

## Changes

We'll update this page and the "Last updated" date if this policy changes.
